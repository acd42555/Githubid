IMG Academy is based in Bradenton Florida and it is a sports academy for student and professional atheletes. People from all around the world come to IMG Academy to train and improve their skills in the sport they play while still focusing on academics.While IMG Academy is mainly focused on the student athelete life style, they also provide a varity of camps for amatures and professionals that like to visit throughout the year. They offer a wide verity of sports including:

  • golf
  • tennis
  • basketball

How I got involved

During the summer of my 10th grade year of High School, I decided to attend a summer camp at IMG Academy for golf. I heard about IMG Academy from my brother who attended a summer camp for tennis while he was still in High School. When I arived at IMG for the camp, I was very imtimidated because this place felt like a college and everybody seemed to be very good at their sport. However, when I finally got adjusted to everything, this place became a dream! I loved every second of that camp and was dying to stay longer. On my last day of camp, I was excited to see my family, but was ] sad that I had to leave. When I was practicing with my coach, he told me that the head director wanted to see me in his office. When I met with him, he told me that my coach was very impressed by me and wanted me to offically stay and play golf for their school. This was one of the best feelings of my life, that I finally got a great oppurtunity to improve my skill level year round at the greatest sports academy in the country.

My Full Time Experience!

I attended IMG Academy my last two years of high school. Throught those two years, I managed to make life long friends from countries all areound the world, train beside the greatest atlelets in the country, and earn a full ride scholorship to play golf for Young Harris College. Those two years really changed my life in more ways than I could every imagine. The IMG Academy experience will allow you to surround yourself with so many people with the same mindset and goals as you. If someone ever has the chance to go to a camp or be lucky enough to go to school their, I would tell them to take full advantage of everything they have to offer and don't waist a second of it.
